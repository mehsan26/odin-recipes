Caprese Salad with Balsamic Glaze

Caprese salad with balsamic glaze is a simple and delicious Italian salad that is typically served as an appetizer or side dish. The salad is composed of fresh, ripe tomatoes, sliced mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves, arranged on a platter or plate. The salad is then seasoned with salt, pepper, and drizzled with high-quality extra-virgin olive oil.

In this particular recipe, the salad is further enhanced with the addition of a balsamic glaze. The balsamic glaze is made by reducing balsamic vinegar and honey to a thick and syrupy consistency, creating a sweet and tangy dressing that perfectly complements the fresh and savory flavors of the salad.

Caprese salad with balsamic glaze is a healthy and light dish that is vegetarian-friendly, gluten-free, and keto-friendly. It is also incredibly versatile and can be customized by using different varieties of tomatoes or cheese, or by adding additional ingredients such as olives or roasted red peppers. This salad is perfect for any occasion, especially during the summer when tomatoes and basil are at their peak.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a large platter, alternating them in a pattern. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine balsamic vinegar and honey. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 3-5 minutes, or until thickened and syrupy.
  3. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the salad and season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional fresh basil leaves if desired.
